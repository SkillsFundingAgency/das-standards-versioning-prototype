Withdraw from a standard, version(s) or the register

End-point assessment organisations (EPAOs) must withdraw if they no longer want to assess a specific standard or be on the register of EPAOs.

We need you to provide some information before you withdraw, so that we can work with you to support any learners.

All withdrawals notifications must comply with the conditions of acceptance (opens in a new tab or window).

Once you withdraw, your organisation cannot reapply to join the register or assess that standard or the same version(s) of that standard for at least 12 months.

What you'll need

You’ll need to tell us:

  • if applicable, the standard / version(s) you wish to withdraw from
  • why you want to withdraw
  • whether you’ll complete your registered learners’ assessments
  • how you’ll communicate your market exit to customers
  • the date that you want to withdraw

You must tell ESFA that you want to withdraw before you tell your customers.
